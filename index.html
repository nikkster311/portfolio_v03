About

Automating the Mundane

I am a Front-End React and Shopify developer based in Washington State. I am passionate about building things that automate the mundane in order to free up time so that you can do things that you actually enjoy doing.

Previously I worked as a park ranger which allowed me to do what I love - be outdoors and experience life in different parts of the country.

The outdoors inspires my work and drives me to build beautiful, thoughtful creations.
