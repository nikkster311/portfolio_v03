About

Automating the Mundane

I am a Front-End React and Shopify developer based in Washington State. I am passionate about building things that automate the mundane in order to free up your time so that you can do the things you actually enjoy doing.

Previously I worked as a park ranger which allowed me to do what I love - be outdoors and experience life in different parts of the country. Through this work I spoke with thousands of visitors a day, from all over world.

The outdoors inspires my work and drives me to build beautiful, thoughtful creations.
